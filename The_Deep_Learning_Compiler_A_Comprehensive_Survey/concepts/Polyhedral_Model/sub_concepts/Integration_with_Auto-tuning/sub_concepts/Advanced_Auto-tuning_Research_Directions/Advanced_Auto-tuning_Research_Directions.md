# Advanced Auto-tuning Research Directions

[[../../Integration_with_Auto-tuning|← Parent]] → [[../../../../../../The_Deep_Learning_Compiler_A_Comprehensive_Survey|📄 The Deep Learning Compiler: A Comprehensive Survey]]

> [!info] Concept (Level 3)
> ==Advanced auto-tuning research directions represent the identified gaps and opportunities for improving current auto-tuning capabilities in DL compilers.== These directions acknowledge that existing auto-tuning approaches have limitations that require further investigation and innovation. The research community recognizes the need for more sophisticated tuning strategies that can handle emerging DL model characteristics and hardware architectures. Advanced auto-tuning may involve techniques like machine learning-guided search, transfer learning across hardware platforms, or integration with polyhedral models. ==This forward-looking perspective helps guide the DL compiler community toward addressing current limitations in optimization automation.==

## Usage in this paper

==The paper highlights advanced auto-tuning as one of several insights for future development of DL compilers.== This positioning indicates that while current auto-tuning is valuable, there is significant room for improvement and innovation. The survey explicitly calls out advanced auto-tuning alongside other future directions like polyhedral models and quantization. This suggests the authors view auto-tuning evolution as critical for next-generation DL compiler capabilities.
